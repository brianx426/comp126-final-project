Ritsurin Garden (栗林公園)

Quick Facts

Ritsurin Park, located in Takamatsu, is one of Japan's most celebrated historical gardens. Ritsurin Park contains stunning and beautiful scenery with its unique setting against the backdrop of Mt. Shiun. The blending of natural landscape with human design makes this park a sight to behold! Ritsurin Park contains 6 tranquil ponds, 13 carefully sculpted hills, and over 1,000 pine trees!

Activities

Ritsurin Park offers a varieties of activities to enjoy. Come partake in these activities and take in the beautiful scenery!


  1. Sip tea and enjoy the view at the Kikugetsu-tei Teahouse, located in the heart of the garden. Enjoy matcha and sweets while gazing out at the serene South Pond. It is said this scene has not changed in centuries!
  2. Take a boat ride across the South Pond in a wasen (traditional wooden boat) guided by boatmen in traditional attire. You'll feel like you were back in the Edo Days!
  3. Each season offers different sights, so be sure to visit every season!
